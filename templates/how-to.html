Welcome to Dublin Bikes!

Here's how you can rent and return a bike to the nearest location using our application.

Renting a Bike

  1. Open the Dublin Bikes application on your device.
  2. Sign in or create an account if you haven't already.
  3. Allow the application to access your location.
  4. On the map, find the nearest Dublin Bikes station that has available bikes.
  5. Tap on the station to see the details and the number of available bikes.
  6. Tap the "Rent" button to select a bike.
  7. Enter the bike number displayed on the bike into the application.
  8. The bike will unlock automatically, and you can start your ride.

Returning a Bike

  1. Find the nearest Dublin Bikes station on the map.
  2. Tap on the station to see the details and the number of available bike docks.
  3. Tap the "Return" button to end your ride.
  4. Lock the bike manually by sliding the lock on the back wheel.
  5. Enter the bike number into the application to confirm the return.
  6. Make sure the bike is securely locked in the dock.
  7. You're all set! You have successfully returned the bike.

Tips for a Smooth Experience

  • Check the bike availability and dock availability before starting your trip to ensure a smooth renting and returning process.
  • Be sure to lock the bike securely in the dock when returning it to avoid any additional charges.
  • If you encounter any issues or have questions, contact the Dublin Bikes customer support for assistance.

Thank you for using the Dublin Bikes application! Happy biking!
